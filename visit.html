On the last day, I visited three places which are a popular tourist attraction in Sokcho.

  • A popular bakery
  • A famous cafe
  • 영랑호

봉브레드

A popular bakery, 봉브레드 is known for such famous bread as 연인의빵 and 마늘바게트. When I visited, there is a long lineup of people in the store. This bakery went viral on SNS as fresh bread. I bought some bread as a kind of souvenir.

너울집

A famous cafe, 너울집 is known for a nice place to take a picture. It also very popular on SNS. Inside there are lots of photo zone. For that reason, many people visit here to take beautiful pictures for their Instagram.

영랑호

영랑호 is the last course I enjoyed before leaving the Sokcho. I was so moved that I couldn't speak. 영랑호 is the most beautiful lake I have ever visited. Brilliant light sparkles on the ripples of the lake.
And I rode a motorbike as a leisure activity. It's really funny!
